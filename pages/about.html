Our Story

Born from Africa's Digital Vision

Founded in 2024 in Mukono, Uganda, Dntech emerged from a simple yet powerful observation: African businesses needed technology partners who truly understood their unique challenges and opportunities.

Our founders, having experienced firsthand the frustrations of working with tech providers who didn't "get" the African context, set out to create something different—a company that combines global technical excellence with deep local market understanding.

Today, we've grown from a small team of passionate technologists to a pan-African force, serving over 500 businesses across 15 countries. But our mission remains unchanged: to bridge the digital divide while celebrating African innovation and expertise.
